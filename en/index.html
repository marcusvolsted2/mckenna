Profit-Driven Marketing

Why we use POAS instead of ROAS

Most agencies optimize for revenue. We optimize for profit - because that is what actually grows your business.

ROAS - Revenue on Ad Spend

ROAS only looks at revenue generated per ad dollar. It ignores the costs that eat into your margin - product costs, shipping, payment fees and discounts.

  • Favors expensive products over high-margin products
  • Can show a great return while you are actually losing money
  • Does not account for promotions, discounts or varying shipping costs

POAS - Profit on Ad Spend

POAS measures actual gross profit against ad spend. A POAS above 1.0 means you are actually profitable - no guesswork, no hidden losses.

  • Directs budget toward products that actually make you money
  • Accounts for all costs: COGS, shipping, payment fees, discounts
  • Clients have saved up to 60% on ad spend while revenue grew
